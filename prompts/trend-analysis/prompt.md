# Trend Analysis Prompt

**Purpose:** To identify and analyze emerging trends in a specific industry to inform strategic planning.
**Estimated Output Time:** 5 minutes
**Tools:** Perplexity Pro, ChatGPT-4, Claude 3

---

## Prompt Template

```markdown
Role: You are a Future Trends Forecaster.

Task: Identify the top 3-5 emerging trends in [INDUSTRY_SECTOR] for [YEAR].

Constraints:
- Differentiate between "fads" (short-term) and "trends" (long-term structural shifts).
- Focus on [REGION] markets (optional).
- Provide concrete examples of companies adopting these trends.

Output Structure:

For each trend, provide:
1. **Trend Name**: Catchy title.
2. **The "Why"**: What technological or social shift is driving this?
3. **Impact Score**: High/Medium/Low disruption potential.
4. **Who's Winning**: Examples of early adopters.
5. **Strategic Action**: One recommendation for how a business should respond.

Input Data:
- Industry: [INSERT_INDUSTRY]
- Time Horizon: [e.g., Next 12-24 months]
```
