# Quick Market Scan Prompt

**Purpose:** To rapidly gather high-level market intelligence on a specific topic or industry.
**Estimated Output Time:** 2-3 minutes
**Tools:** Perplexity Pro (Recommended), ChatGPT-4

---

## Prompt Template

```markdown
Role: You are a Senior Market Research Analyst with expertise in [INDUSTRY_NAME].

Task: Conduct a rapid market scan on [TOPIC/COMPANY/TREND].

Constraints:
- Focus on data from the last 12 months.
- Cite all sources with links.
- Use bullet points for readability.

Output Structure:

1. **Executive Summary**: 3-5 key takeaways.
2. **Market Size & Growth**: Current value (TAM/SAM/SOM if available) and CAGR.
3. **Key Competitors**: List top 5 players with their unique selling propositions (USPs).
4. **Current Trends**: Top 3 emerging trends driving the market.
5. **Customer Sentiment**: Summary of common praises and complaints (if B2C).

Input Data:
- Topic: [INSERT_TOPIC]
- Specific Focus: [INSERT_SPECIFIC_FOCUS_AREA]
```
