# Competitor Deep Dive Prompt

**Purpose:** To conduct a comprehensive analysis of a competitor's business model, product strategy, and market positioning.
**Estimated Output Time:** 5-10 minutes
**Tools:** Perplexity Pro (Recommended), ChatGPT-4

---

## Prompt Template

```markdown
Role: You are a Strategic Competitor Analyst.

Task: Perform a deep dive analysis of [COMPETITOR_NAME].

Constraints:
- Use data from the last 6-12 months.
- Compare against [YOUR_COMPANY_OR_BENCHMARK] where possible.
- Cite financial reports, press releases, and user reviews.

Output Structure:

1. **Company Overview**: Founded, HQ, Key Executives, Estimated Revenue.
2. **Business Model**: How do they make money? (Subscription, Ads, Enterprise sales, etc.)
3. **Product Analysis**:
   - Core Features
   - Pricing Tiers (Table format)
   - Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
4. **SWOT Analysis**:
   - Strengths (Internal)
   - Weaknesses (Internal)
   - Opportunities (External)
   - Threats (External)
5. **Customer Sentiment**:
   - Top 3 things users love (aggregating G2/Capterra reviews)
   - Top 3 things users hate

Input Data:
- Competitor: [INSERT_COMPETITOR_NAME]
- Focus Area: [OPTIONAL_SPECIFIC_PRODUCT_LINE]
```
